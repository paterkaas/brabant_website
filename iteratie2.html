⚠️ ETA & EES — sort it early, it is simple

Since October 2025, British travellers need an ETA to enter the Netherlands. It takes about 10 minutes online and costs £10 per person. Do it at least two weeks before travel — some have had the app crash on passport scans. Done early, genuinely stress-free.

💡 Paying in the Netherlands

Most Dutch supermarkets do not accept British Visa credit cards. Maestro debit cards and Mastercard work fine, or bring euros in cash as a backup.

💬 Language

Almost everyone in Noord-Brabant speaks excellent English. Automated systems like parking meters may default to Dutch — just tap around.
